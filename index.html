Welcome to Veg-O-Rama!

Veg-O-Rama is a Fast Casual Vegetarian Food restaurant located in Ypsilanti, MI. We offer a wide variety of delicious and healthy burgers, wraps, sides and snacks. Each item on our menu is carefully crafted and packed with interesting Indo-American fusion, flavors and fresh ingredients. Whether you are a vegetarian, vegan or just curious, this “Love Food” brings your cravings to Life!
