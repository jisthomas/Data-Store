About Us

  • Everything we make starts from the heart.

    Our mission at Veg-O-Rama is to serve Indo-American “Love Food” packed with interesting flavors, fusion and fresh ingredients. Cooked with warm heart and served with smile, we offer variety of food which will bring your cravings to Life!

  • We Have many options for All

    At Veg-O-Rama we have the belief that vegetarian options can be for everyone. Not just a vegetarian or vegan. It's our goal to introduce a different style and healthy options to community at very affordable price.

  • Be part of Our Community

    Every year we host Colorfest Michigan event which is also known as Happiest event on the planet. We also conduct International vegetarian Festival every year to promote healthy living style. In the future we plan on hosting such events at Ypsilanti with more cooking classes, Ayurveda/Yoga seminars and more. We are also planning to host “Yoga-O-Rama” festival this summer at Ypsilanti. And this is only the beginning! We want the Ypsilanti Community to be a part of our Healthy and Happy community and see what it is we do. Keep coming back to the website not just to order but to see updates on all the upcoming events we are going to be hosting!
